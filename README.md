# Phys box

on click down it summons the objects to a square around mouse, you can drag, releasing click causes explosion
keys 1 through 5 change the "bouncieness" attribute of the objects, 1 being the bounciest and 5 being no bounce

holding space down makes the objects multi coloured, on release, objects return to white

holding 8 makes the objects flash multi coloured, on release, objects return to white


objects register the left, right, and bottom borders of the screen as physical, they do not go through them/may bounce
